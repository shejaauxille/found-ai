How It Works

1

Registration

Families upload photos of missing loved ones. Our AI extracts unique facial "descriptors" to store in a secure local database.

2

AI Scanning

When someone is found, a photo is uploaded. The AI compares 68 facial landmarks against the registered database in real-time.

3

Verification

The system calculates a mathematical distance between faces. A match is triggered if the similarity exceeds 94% accuracy.

4

Reunification

Upon a match, EmailJS automatically exchanges contact details between the family and the finder to facilitate a safe return.
